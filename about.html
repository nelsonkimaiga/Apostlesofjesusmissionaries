The Apostles of Jesus Congregation


About the Apostles of Jesus Missionaries :

Co-founded in 1976 by Rev Bishop Sixtus Mazzoldi and Rev Fr. John Marengoni of the Comboni Missionaries, The Apostles of Jesus congregation is a mission founded to nurture vocational training to seminarians in the sacrament of religious Holy Orders. Throughout the entire journey , we are driven by virtues in the domain of Prayer, Study and Work in our various instutions of learning which include Junior Seminary all th way to Major Seminary.
